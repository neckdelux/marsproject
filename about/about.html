Our Mars Mission

SpaceX was founded with the ultimate goal of enabling human life on Mars. Our Mars program aims to establish the first sustainable human settlement on the Red Planet by 2030, making humanity a multi-planetary species.

Using our revolutionary Starship spacecraft, we plan to transport the first pioneers to Mars within this decade, beginning the process of building a permanent human presence.

Mission Timeline

2025

First Cargo Missions

Initial unmanned supply missions delivering infrastructure components to Mars surface.

2026

Construction Begins

Robotic systems begin constructing habitation modules and life support infrastructure.

2027

Energy Systems

Deployment of solar arrays and nuclear power systems for sustainable energy generation.

Historic arrival of first human crew to begin permanent settlement operations.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to travel to Mars?

+

The journey to Mars takes approximately 6-8 months using our current propulsion technology. Future advancements may reduce this travel time.

How will humans survive on Mars?

+

Our habitation modules include advanced life support systems, water recycling, and oxygen generation. Initial settlements will use a combination of shipped supplies and locally produced resources.

Can I apply to be a Mars colonist?

+

Yes! We are currently accepting applications for our Mars Pioneer Program. Candidates should have expertise in fields such as engineering, medicine, agriculture, or construction.

What is the long-term vision for Mars?

+

Our long-term vision includes establishing a self-sustaining city of one million people on Mars, with its own economy and manufacturing capabilities, possibly leading to terraforming efforts in the far future.
